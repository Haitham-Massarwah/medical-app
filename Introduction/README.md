## 💡 **Tips for Effective Presentations**

### **Do:**
- ✅ Start with doctor's pain points
- ✅ Show, don't just tell (use demo)
- ✅ Speak in benefits, not features
- ✅ Address privacy/security concerns
- ✅ Offer trial period

### **Don't:**
- ❌ Use too much technical jargon
- ❌ Rush through the demo
- ❌ Ignore doctor's specific concerns
- ❌ Oversell or overpromise
- ❌ Forget to follow up
