## 💡 **Pro Tips**

### **Do's:**
- ✅ Always start with the doctor's pain point
- ✅ Use specific numbers and examples
- ✅ Show, don't just tell (demo is key)
- ✅ Build trust through testimonials
- ✅ Make it risk-free (trial period)
- ✅ Follow up persistently but politely

### **Don'ts:**
- ❌ Use technical jargon
- ❌ Oversell or exaggerate
- ❌ Rush the conversation
- ❌ Ignore their specific concerns
- ❌ Give up after first "no"
- ❌ Forget to ask for referrals
