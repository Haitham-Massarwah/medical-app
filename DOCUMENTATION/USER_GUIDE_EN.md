**Web Browser:**

2. Go to: `https://your-clinic-domain.com`
3. The homepage will display medical specialties

**Mobile App:**
1. Download from App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
2. Install and open the app
3. Grant necessary permissions

**Desktop App:**
1. Download for Windows or macOS
2. Install and launch
3. Sign in or continue as guest

## 👤 For Patients

### Creating an Account

**Option 1: Self-Registration (if enabled)**
1. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**
2. Fill in your details:
   - First name and last name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the link to verify your email

**Option 2: Doctor Creates Your Profile**
1. Your doctor will create your profile
2. You'll receive an email with a registration link
3. Click the link and complete your profile
4. Set your password
5. You're ready to book!

### Booking an Appointment

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Select Specialty**
   - On the homepage, click a medical specialty card
   - Examples: Osteopath, Dentist, Massage, Physiotherapy

2. **Choose Provider**
   - Browse the list of available providers
   - View their:
     - Experience and qualifications
     - Languages spoken
     - Location
     - Reviews and ratings
     - Price range
   - Click **"Book Appointment"**

3. **Select Time**
   - View the calendar with available slots
   - Green slots = available
   - Grey slots = booked
   - Click your preferred time
   - Use quick buttons: Today, Tomorrow, This Week

4. **Confirm Details**
   - Review appointment information
   - Add any notes or special requests
   - Choose: In-person or Telehealth (video)

5. **Payment**
   - Choose payment method:
     - Credit/Debit card
     - Bank transfer
     - Pay at clinic
   - Pay deposit or full amount (per clinic policy)
   - Enter card details securely

6. **Confirmation**
   - You'll see a success message
   - Receive confirmation email
   - Optional: SMS/WhatsApp confirmation
   - Appointment appears in "My Appointments"

### Managing Your Appointments

**View Appointments:**
1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select **"My Appointments"**
3. You'll see:
   - **Upcoming** - Future appointments
   - **Past** - Completed appointments
   - **Cancelled** - Cancelled appointments

**Cancel Appointment:**
1. Go to "My Appointments"
2. Find the appointment
3. Click **"Cancel"**
4. Provide a reason (optional)
5. Confirm cancellation
6. Note: Cancellation fees may apply based on clinic policy

**Reschedule Appointment:**
1. Go to "My Appointments"
2. Find the appointment
3. Click **"Reschedule"**
4. Select new date and time
5. Confirm changes
6. Receive updated confirmation

**Confirm Attendance:**
1. You'll receive a reminder 2 hours before
2. Click **"Confirm Attendance"** in the message
3. Or confirm in the app
4. This helps reduce no-shows

### Your Profile

**Update Profile:**
1. Click profile icon → **"Profile"**
2. Edit:
   - Personal information
   - Contact details
   - Emergency contact
   - Medical information (allergies, medications)
   - Insurance details
3. Click **"Save"**

**Upload Documents:**
1. Go to **"Documents"**
2. Click **"Upload"**
3. Select file (PDF, JPG, PNG)
4. Add description
5. Click **"Upload"**

**Payment History:**
1. Go to **"Payments"**
2. View all transactions
3. Download receipts
4. Request refunds (if applicable)

## 👨‍⚕️ For Doctors

### Setting Up Your Profile

**Complete Your Profile:**
1. Log in with credentials provided by admin
2. Go to **"My Profile"**
3. Add:
   - Profile photo
   - Specialty
   - License number
   - Education
   - Languages spoken
   - Bio/description
4. Click **"Save"**

### Managing Availability

**Set Regular Hours:**
1. Go to **"Calendar"** → **"Availability"**
2. For each day of the week:
   - Toggle day on/off
   - Set start time (e.g., 9:00 AM)
   - Set end time (e.g., 5:00 PM)
3. Set appointment duration (default: 30 minutes)
4. Set buffer time between appointments
5. Click **"Save"**

**Add Exceptions:**
1. Go to **"Calendar"** → **"Exceptions"**
2. Click **"Add Exception"**
3. Select date
4. Choose type:
   - **Blocked** - Not available (vacation, holiday)
   - **Available** - Extra hours (special availability)
5. Set time range (or leave empty for full day)
6. Add reason/note
7. Click **"Save"**

**Israeli Holidays:**
- System pre-loads Israeli holidays
- You can enable/disable auto-blocking for holidays
- Customize which holidays to block

### Managing Appointments

**View Your Schedule:**
1. Go to **"Calendar"**
2. Switch views:
   - **Day** - Hour-by-hour schedule
   - **Week** - Week overview
   - **Month** - Monthly view
3. Click appointment to see details

**Appointment Actions:**
- **Approve** - If manual approval is enabled
- **Reject** - Decline appointment with reason
- **Reschedule** - Propose new time
- **Mark as Complete** - After visit
- **Mark as No-Show** - If patient doesn't attend
- **Add Notes** - Clinical notes (private)

### Creating Patient Profiles

**Add New Patient:**
1. Go to **"Patients"** → **"Add Patient"**
2. Enter basic information:
   - Name, email, phone
3. Click **"Create"**
4. Patient receives registration link to complete profile

**Send Registration Link:**
1. Find patient in list
2. Click **"Send Registration Link"**
3. Patient receives email with secure link
4. They complete their profile themselves

### Managing Services

**Add Treatment Type:**
1. Go to **"Services"** → **"Add Service"**
2. Enter details:
   - Service name (e.g., "Osteopathy Session")
   - Description
   - Duration (minutes)
   - Price (ILS)
3. Toggle active/inactive
4. Click **"Save"**

**Examples of Treatment Types:**
- Osteopathy consultation - 60 min - ₪300
- Physiotherapy session - 45 min - ₪250
- Dental cleaning - 30 min - ₪200
- Massage therapy - 60 min - ₪350
- Acupuncture - 45 min - ₪280

### Sending Reminders

**Manual Reminders:**
1. Go to appointment details
2. Click **"Send Reminder"**
3. Choose channel: Email, SMS, or WhatsApp
4. Edit message if needed
5. Click **"Send"**

**Automatic Reminders:**

### Viewing Reports

**Access Reports:**
1. Go to **"Reports"**
2. Select report type:

## 🔄 Common Tasks

### How to: Find a Doctor

1. Go to homepage
2. Use search bar or select specialty
3. Apply filters:
   - Location/distance
   - Language
   - Gender
   - Price range
   - Availability
   - Telehealth option
4. Browse results
5. Click **"View Profile"** for details
6. Click **"Book Appointment"** when ready

### How to: Book a Telehealth Appointment

1. Find provider who offers telehealth
2. Look for 🎥 video icon
3. Select time slot
4. Choose **"Telehealth/Video"** option
5. Complete booking
6. You'll receive video link 15 minutes before appointment
7. Click link to join at appointment time

### How to: Cancel Within Policy

1. Go to **"My Appointments"**
2. Find appointment
3. Check cancellation deadline (shown on card)
4. If within free cancellation window:
   - Click **"Cancel"**
   - Provide reason
   - Confirm
   - Get full refund
5. If outside window:
   - See penalty amount
   - Confirm cancellation
   - Partial refund processed

### How to: Upload Medical Documents

1. Go to **"Documents"** or **"Medical Records"**
2. Click **"Upload Document"**
3. Choose file (PDF, JPG, PNG - max 10MB)
4. Add details:
   - Document type (Lab results, X-ray, etc.)
   - Date
   - Description
5. Click **"Upload"**
6. Document available to your providers

### How to: Request Refund

1. Go to **"Payments"**
2. Find transaction
3. Click **"Request Refund"**
4. Provide reason
5. Submit request
6. Admin reviews and approves
7. Refund processed within 5-10 business days

### How to: Change Password

1. Click profile icon → **"Settings"**
2. Go to **"Security"**
3. Click **"Change Password"**
4. Enter current password
5. Enter new password
6. Confirm new password
7. Click **"Update Password"**

### How to: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Security"**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes securely
6. Click **"Confirm"**
7. 2FA is now enabled!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Solutions:**
1. Check email and password are correct
2. Try **"Forgot Password"** to reset
3. Check if email is verified (check spam folder)
4. Try different browser
5. Clear browser cache and cookies

### Appointment Slots Not Showing

**Reasons:**
- Doctor hasn't set availability
- Selected date is in the past
- Doctor is fully booked
- Clinic is closed on that day

**Solutions:**
- Try different date
- Try different provider
- Contact clinic directly
- Use "Notify me when available" feature

### Payment Failed

**Common Causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Incorrect card details
- Bank declined transaction
- 3D Secure verification failed

**Solutions:**
- Try different card
- Contact your bank
- Use alternative payment method
- Pay at clinic instead

### Not Receiving Notifications

**Check:**
1. Go to **"Settings"** → **"Notifications"**
2. Verify notifications are enabled
3. Check email/phone number is correct
4. Check spam/junk folder
5. Verify phone number format (+972...)

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is my medical information secure?**  
A: Yes. All data is encrypted, and we comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and Israeli Privacy Law.

**Q: Can I book for family members?**  
A: Yes, if your doctor creates their profiles or you add them as dependents.

**Q: Do I need an account to book?**  
A: Guest booking is available (if enabled by clinic). Account recommended for history tracking.

**Q: Which languages are supported?**  
A: Hebrew, Arabic, and English with full RTL support.

### Appointments

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**  
A: Typically up to 90 days in advance (set by clinic).

**Q: What's the minimum notice for booking?**  
A: Usually 2 hours before appointment time (set by clinic).

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Cancellation policies vary by clinic. Check your appointment details for specific rules.

**Q: What if I'm late?**  
A: Contact the clinic immediately. Most providers have a 10-15 minute grace period.

**Q: What is a no-show fee?**  
A: If you don't show up and don't cancel, you may be charged per clinic policy.

### Payments

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: Either when booking (deposit/full) or after appointment, per clinic policy.

**Q: Is my card information stored?**  
A: No. We use Stripe tokenization. No card details are stored on our servers.

**Q: How do refunds work?**  
A: Refunds are automatic based on cancellation policy. Processed within 5-10 business days.

**Q: Can I get an invoice?**  
A: Yes. All payments include receipts/invoices, sent to your email.

### Telehealth

**Q: What do I need for video appointments?**  
A: Webcam, microphone, stable internet connection, and a modern browser.

**Q: How do I join a video appointment?**  
A: You'll receive a link 15 minutes before. Click it at appointment time.

**Q: What if video doesn't work?**  
A: Try different browser, check internet connection, or call clinic for phone appointment.
