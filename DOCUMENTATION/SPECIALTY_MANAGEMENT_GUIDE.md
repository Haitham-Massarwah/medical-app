# 🎯 **SPECIALTY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM**

## ✅ **HOW IT WORKS:**

### **1. Developer Control** 🛠️
- **Go to Developer Dashboard** → "ניהול התמחויות"
- **Select which specialties** to show customers
- **Organized by categories** with emojis:
  - 🦷 **Dental & Oral Care** (7 specialties)
  - 💆 **Physical Therapy & Body Treatments** (11 specialties)
  - 💅 **Aesthetic & Beauty Treatments** (6 specialties)
  - 🌿 **Complementary & Holistic Medicine** (8 specialties)
  - 🧘 **Wellness & Rehabilitation** (7 specialties)
  - 🧠 **Mental Health & Emotional Support** (8 specialties)
  - 👂 **Sensory & Communication Therapies** (4 specialties)
  - 👶 **Family & Women's Health** (6 specialties)
  - 🦾 **Rehabilitation & Medical Support** (4 specialties)
  - **General Medical** (8 specialties)

### **2. Customer Experience** 👥
- **Customers see only** developer-selected specialties
- **Doctors list dropdown** shows filtered specialties
- **Real-time updates** when developer changes selection

### **3. Marketing Control** 📈
- **Developer can focus** on specific medical areas
- **Seasonal specialties** (e.g., only dental in December)
- **Market-specific** specialties based on demand
- **A/B testing** different specialty combinations

## 🚀 **TEST THE SYSTEM:**

### **Step 1: Login as Developer**
1. **Login** with any email/password
2. **Choose "מפתח" (Developer)**
3. **Go to "ניהול התמחויות"**

### **Step 2: Select Specialties**
1. **Uncheck categories** you don't want
2. **Check specific specialties** within categories
3. **Click "שמור הגדרות"**
4. **See confirmation** with count

### **Step 3: Test Customer View**
1. **Switch to "מטופל" (Patient)**
2. **Go to "רופאים" (Doctors)**
3. **Check dropdown** - only shows selected specialties
4. **Search works** with filtered list
