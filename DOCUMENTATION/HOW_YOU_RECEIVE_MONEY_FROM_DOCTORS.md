## 🎯 The Complete Flow

### When a Doctor Signs Up:

1. **Doctor Registers** (Free)
   - Creates account with email/password
   - Fills in profile information
   - Account created ✅

2. **Doctor Tries to Use Platform** 
   - Tries to book appointments
   - System blocks them ❌
   - Shows: "**Subscription Required**"

3. **Doctor Chooses a Plan**
   - Sees 3 plans: Basic (₪99), Professional (₪199), Enterprise (₪399)
   - All include 14-day FREE trial
   - Selects a plan

4. **Doctor Enters Payment Details**
   - Enters credit card information
   - Stripe securely stores card (PCI compliant)
   - **NO CHARGE during 14-day trial**

5. **Trial Period (14 Days)**
   - Doctor gets full access
   - Can use all features
   - No charges yet
   - Can cancel anytime

6. **After Trial (Day 15)**
   - **Stripe automatically charges the card**
   - Money goes to YOUR Stripe account
   - Doctor gets invoice via email
   - Continues with full access ✅

7. **Every Month After**
   - **Stripe charges automatically** on the same day
   - Money deposited to YOUR bank account
   - Doctor gets invoice
   - YOU DO NOTHING - It's automatic! 🎉

## 🚨 What If Payment Fails?

Stripe handles this automatically too!

### Automatic Process:

1. **Payment fails** (expired card, insufficient funds)
2. **Stripe retries** - 4 attempts over 2 weeks
3. **Email sent** to doctor: "Payment failed, update card"
4. **System marks** subscription as "past_due"
5. **Access limited** until payment succeeds
6. **You get notification** in Stripe Dashboard

### You Can:
- See failed payments in dashboard
- Email doctor to update payment method
- Offer to help with payment issues
- Track which subscriptions are at risk
