# 🧭 How to Navigate to OAuth Client ID Page

## ❌ You're Currently On:
- **IAM & Admin** → **Service accounts** → **Create service account**
- This is WRONG for calendar integration!

---

## ✅ Where You Need to Go:

### Step-by-Step Navigation:

1. **Click the back arrow** (←) at the top left of the page
   - OR click "Google Cloud Platform Home" logo

2. **In the left sidebar**, look for:
   - **"APIs & services"** (NOT "IAM & Admin")
   - Click on **"APIs & services"**

3. **In the left menu under "APIs & services"**, click:
   - **"Credentials"**

4. **On the Credentials page:**
   - Look at the top of the page
   - Click the blue button: **"+ Create Credentials"**
   - A dropdown menu will appear

5. **From the dropdown:**
   - Select **"OAuth client ID"**
   - (NOT "Service account key" or "Service account")

---
